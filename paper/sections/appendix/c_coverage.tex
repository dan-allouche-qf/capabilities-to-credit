\section{Coverage matrix}\label{app:coverage}

The full coverage matrix --- one cell per (country, indicator) pair, value = share of years observed over 1990--2024 --- is in \texttt{outputs/tables/coverage\_matrix.csv}. \Cref{fig:coverage} in the main text visualises it. Countries are ordered by average coverage. Cells with coverage below 0.30 are excluded from the within-sector MICE imputation step.
