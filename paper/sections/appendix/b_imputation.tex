\section{Imputation diagnostics}\label{app:imputation}

The tiered imputation pipeline (Section~\ref{sec:data}) takes the harmonised raw panel (41\,671 non-null cells observed) and produces an imputed panel of 59\,570 cells. The pipeline therefore fills $59\,570 - 41\,671 = 17\,899$ cells. The breakdown by tier (linear interpolation across interior gaps, forward-fill of recent years, and within-sector MICE) is not recorded as a separate counter in the current build; the totals above are reported as the net pipeline contribution. Removing the imputed panel and re-running the headline results on the raw panel costs less than half a percentage point of OOS AUC and drops the 2024 composite Spearman correlation against the imputed-panel ranking to $0.862$ (Section~\ref{sec:robustness}). Cells left as \texttt{NaN} after the pipeline runs are concentrated in AES Sahel R\&I in the 1990s and Russia 2022 (the rating-withdrawal window).
